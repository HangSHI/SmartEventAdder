Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Smart Event Adder collects and processes the following information:

  • Email Content: We read email content to extract event information (dates, times, locations)
  • Google Account Info: Basic profile information (email address) for authentication
  • Calendar Access: Permission to create events in your Google Calendar

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Parse emails for event-related information
  • Create calendar events based on extracted information
  • Provide the core functionality of the Smart Event Adder

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Email content is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Only event-related data is temporarily cached for processing
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We follow Google's security best practices

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Google services (Gmail, Calendar) as required for functionality
  • When required by law or legal process

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Email content is not stored permanently.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke access permissions at any time through Google Account settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Contact us for questions about your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
