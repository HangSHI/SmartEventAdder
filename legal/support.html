Getting Started

Smart Event Adder helps you automatically create calendar events from your Gmail messages.

  1. Install the add-on from Google Workspace Marketplace
  2. Open any email in Gmail
  3. Look for the Smart Event Adder icon in the right sidebar
  4. Click the icon to see suggested events from your email
  5. Review and add events to your calendar

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does Smart Event Adder work?

A: The add-on uses AI to analyze your email content and identify event-related information like dates, times, locations, and event descriptions. It then suggests calendar events based on this information.

Q: Is my email data secure?

A: Yes! We only process email content to extract event information. Email content is not permanently stored, and all data transmission is encrypted.

Q: Why aren't events being detected in my emails?

A: Event detection works best when emails contain clear date/time information. Make sure emails include specific dates, times, or phrases like "meeting," "appointment," or "event."

Q: Can I customize the event details?

A: Yes! Before adding events to your calendar, you can review and modify the title, time, date, and location as needed.

Q: How do I uninstall the add-on?

A: Go to Gmail Settings → Add-ons → Smart Event Adder → Remove, or manage it through your Google Account settings.

Troubleshooting

Add-on not appearing in Gmail sidebar

A: Try refreshing Gmail, clearing browser cache, or checking if the add-on is enabled in Gmail Settings → Add-ons.

Events not being added to calendar

A: Ensure you have granted calendar permissions to the add-on. Check Google Account Settings → Security → Third-party apps.

Performance issues

A: Close other browser tabs, disable other Gmail add-ons temporarily, or try using a different browser.
